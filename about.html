WHo we are?

Our Mission

To return marriage to God through the following ways;

  • Hosting His Presence in homes through the family altars
  • Prayer
  • Reading and meditating on the Word of God (both written and revealed)
  • Living in obedience and submission to the will of God

Our Vision

To see marriage and family restored to the original plan of God at Creation.

Our Core Values

Love and forgiveness, Honour, Patience, Obedience, Service above self, Humility, Accountability, Commitment to God, Unity, Fellowship and Communion, Submission to Established Authority, Truthfulness, Excellence, Faithfulness and Diligence

Your Family Network

The name: "My Place of Rest"

Is inspired from Malachi 2:11: Marriage is an institution that he loves, and John 15:4: The Lord God almighty desires to abide in our marriages and us in him. Marriage is the Lord's sanctuary- The place where He rests.
