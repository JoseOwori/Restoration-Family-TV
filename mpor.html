My Place of Rest Ministries

Brief History

My Place of Rest Ministries was founded with a passion to restore families and marriages to their God-given purpose. The ministry began as a small prayer group, meeting in homes and seeking God's guidance for families facing challenges. Over time, it grew into a vibrant community, reaching out to many through teaching, counseling, and outreach programs.

How the Ministry Started

The ministry started when a group of devoted believers recognized the need for spiritual support and restoration in families. Inspired by scripture and a calling to serve, they began organizing family altars, prayer meetings, and workshops focused on healing and strengthening relationships. Their dedication laid the foundation for what would become My Place of Rest Ministries.

Meet The Vision Bearer

Pastor Margaret M. Magera

The Vision Bearer of My Place of Rest Ministries is Pastor Margaret M. Magera, whose heart for families and unwavering faith have been instrumental in shaping the ministry's direction. With years of experience in pastoral care and a deep commitment to God's word, Pastor Margaret leads with compassion, wisdom, and a vision to see families transformed by God's love.
